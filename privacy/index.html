Harbour Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Harbour is a browser extension that lets users save supported web pages and videos to their own Harbour server.

Information We Collect

The extension does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

When you choose to save a page, only the information required to save that page is sent to your own configured Harbour server.

Data Storage

Your saved items are stored only on the Harbour server that you configure.

Third Parties

Harbour does not sell or share your personal information with third parties.
